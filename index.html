Quality Care

Hunsaker Home encourages resident choice and independence, while offering personalized care and services to assist with needs that cannot be met independently.

  • Personal care
  • Bowel and bladder care
  • Transfers and mobility
  • Meals and housekeeping
  • Medication management
  • Behavioral management
  • Life-enhancing activities

Choose Hunsaker

Our "Target Group" includes individuals who meet specific criteria upon admission, such as eligibility for Medicaid Long-Term Care Services. Many require full assistance in mobility, toileting, eating, or cognition.
